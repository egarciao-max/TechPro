About TechPRO

From personal laptops to small business workstations, TechPRO Computer Repair offers complete repair and maintenance services. We handle everything from hardware upgrades and screen replacements to software troubleshooting and system tune-ups. In times of crisis, you can count on us for crucial data recovery and virus removal, ensuring your precious files and personal information are kept safe.

Hands-On Expertise, Inside and Out

We believe that every device has a story and that most problems have a solution. Our technicians take a detailed, hands-on approach to every repair. We don't just fix the symptom; we dive deep to find the root cause, whether it's a faulty component on the motherboard or a hidden software conflict.

This commitment to thorough, precise work means you get a repair that lasts. We treat your technology with the care it deserves, ensuring it returns to you in peak condition.
